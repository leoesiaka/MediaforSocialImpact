Why This Fellowship Matters

For storytellers who want to build something bigger than content.

This fellowship supports people who care about using media responsibly and creatively to influence conversations, inform communities, and drive positive change.

  • Storytelling that informs and transforms.
  • Leadership development for emerging media voices.
  • Access to a virtual global learning community.
  • Recognition, support, and practical project growth.

Eligibility & Benefits

Who Can Apply and What You Will Gain

This cohort is built for emerging storytellers who want to grow in craft, leadership, and public impact.

Who Can Apply?

  • Age 18 – 40 years
  • Passionate about storytelling and social impact
  • Journalists, filmmakers, content creators, podcasters, bloggers, photographers, media entrepreneurs, or communication professionals
  • Demonstrate leadership potential
  • Have access to reliable internet
  • Willing to actively participate in a virtual program

What You Will Gain

  • Expert-led training and masterclasses
  • Mentorship from global media leaders
  • Leadership and personal development
  • Access to a global alumni network
  • Collaboration on real-world media projects
  • Recognition and publication opportunities
